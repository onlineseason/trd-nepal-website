# PROJECT DESCRIPTION

TRD Nepal is a comprehensive study abroad consulting platform designed to help Nepalese students achieve their international education dreams. The website serves as a hub for:

1. **Study Abroad Counseling** - Expert guidance on university selection and application strategies
2. **Test Preparation** - Coaching for standardized tests (SAT, IELTS, TOEFL, GRE, GMAT, PTE, ACT, Duolingo)
3. **SOP & Essay Writing** - Professional statement of purpose and essay writing services
4. **Scholarship Guidance** - Assistance finding and securing global scholarships
5. **Visa & Immigration Support** - Complete visa application assistance
6. **Interview Preparation** - Coaching for university and visa interviews
